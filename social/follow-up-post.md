# LinkedIn Follow-Up Post

The most useful AI exercise I have found is brutally simple:

Write down what you actually did yesterday.

Then ask:

Which parts of this should never require a blank page again?

That question is different from "what can AI do?"

It starts from the work.

The meeting recap.

The follow-up draft.

The scorecard explanation.

The coaching prep.

The tool review.

The founder feedback.

The public post that turns private learning into shared judgment.

That is what became the Job-To-AI Skill Challenge.

Not a course.

Not a prompt pack.

A way to turn your job into reusable leverage.

If you had to build 12 AI skills from your actual workday, what would make the list?
