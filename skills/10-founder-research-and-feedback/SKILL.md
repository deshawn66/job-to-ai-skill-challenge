# Founder Research And Feedback

Turn product testing into a useful point of view.

## Inputs

- Startup or product name
- Website or product notes
- User's role
- Job task tested
- What worked
- What failed
- What would make it useful

## Workflow

1. Summarize the product promise in plain English.
2. Compare the promise to the user's real job task.
3. Separate useful, hype, missing, and worth-paying-for.
4. Write a short opinion the user can share publicly.
5. Draft a specific, respectful founder message.

## Output

Return:

- Product summary
- Useful parts
- Hype or weak spots
- Missing workflow pieces
- Would-pay-if condition
- Founder message
- Optional public scorecard summary

## Safety

- Do not pretend the user tested features they did not test.
- Do not insult founders.
- Do not share private company data in feedback.
- If current product details matter, verify them before making claims.
