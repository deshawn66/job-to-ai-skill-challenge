# Skill 10: Founder Research And Feedback

## What It Does

Researches an AI startup and turns the trial into useful founder feedback.

## Who It Helps

Operators who want to form a real opinion instead of repeating AI hype.

## Inputs Needed

- Startup name
- Website
- Product category
- Your job role
- Trial notes
- What you hoped it would solve

## Tools It May Interact With

- Search engine
- Product website
- Notes
- LinkedIn
- Email

## Prompt

Review this AI tool from the perspective of my actual job. Separate what is useful, what is hype, what is missing, what I would pay for, and what feedback the founder should hear. Draft a short founder message that is specific, respectful, and actionable.

## Sample Input

I tried an AI tool for sales follow-up. It created generic messages, but it did not understand our approval steps or when a human should review the message.

## Sample Output

Useful:

- Fast first drafts.
- Good for simple check-ins.

Missing:

- No approval workflow.
- Weak understanding of regulated sales language.
- No way to connect to real performance context.

Founder message:

Hi [Name], I tried [Tool] from the perspective of a sales operator. The fast drafts are useful, but the product would be much stronger if it had an approval step before anything customer-facing goes out. In regulated industries, draft-only mode is a feature, not a limitation.

## Safety Notes

- Do not pretend you tested features you did not test.
- Do not insult founders.
- Do not share private company data in feedback.
