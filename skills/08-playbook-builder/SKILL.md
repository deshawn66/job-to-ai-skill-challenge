# Playbook Builder

Turn repeatable work into a usable playbook.

## Inputs

- Goal of the playbook
- Audience
- Current process notes
- Examples of good work
- Common mistakes
- Desired action after reading

## Workflow

1. Clarify who the playbook is for and when they should use it.
2. Write the goal in plain English.
3. Turn the process into clear steps.
4. Add examples and common mistakes.
5. End with a checklist someone can use immediately.

## Output

Return:

- Title
- Goal
- Audience
- When to use it
- Step-by-step process
- Examples
- Common mistakes
- Checklist

## Safety

- Do not make unsupported claims.
- Do not include private examples in public playbooks.
- For regulated industries, flag claims that need review.
- Keep the language usable by someone who has not heard the live explanation.
