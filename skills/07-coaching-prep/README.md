# Skill 07: Coaching Prep

## What It Does

Prepares a focused coaching conversation.

## Who It Helps

Managers, sales leaders, and team leads.

## Inputs Needed

- Person's role
- Current performance snapshot
- Recent activity
- Desired outcome
- Any sensitive context

## Tools It May Interact With

- Performance dashboard
- Notes
- Calendar
- Training library
- Sales activity tracker

## Prompt

Prepare a coaching conversation for this person. Start with the goal of the conversation, then list what to affirm, what to challenge, what questions to ask, and what next commitment to request. Keep the tone direct, respectful, and practical.

## Sample Input

A sales professional is below goal but has improved activity over the last two weeks. I want to encourage the effort while making the next standard clear.

## Sample Output

Goal:

Recognize the improved activity and turn it into a clear next commitment.

Affirm:

- You increased your activity over the last two weeks.
- That tells me you are not checked out.

Challenge:

- Activity is good, but we need it connected to appointments and funded business.

Questions:

- What activity is creating real conversations?
- Where are you getting stuck after the first contact?

Next commitment:

Agree on a specific weekly activity target and one conversion skill to practice.

## Safety Notes

- Do not diagnose personal issues.
- Do not write harsh feedback without context.
- Do not expose employee performance data publicly.
