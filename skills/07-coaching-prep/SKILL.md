# Coaching Prep

Prepare a focused coaching conversation.

## Inputs

- Person's role
- Current performance or behavior snapshot
- Recent activity or context
- Desired outcome
- Any sensitive context

## Workflow

1. Define the goal of the conversation.
2. Identify what to affirm.
3. Identify what to challenge.
4. Write questions that create ownership.
5. Recommend one clear next commitment.
6. Keep the tone direct, respectful, and practical.

## Output

Return:

- Conversation goal
- What to affirm
- What to challenge
- Questions to ask
- Recommended next commitment
- Optional opening script

## Safety

- Do not diagnose personal issues.
- Do not write harsh feedback without context.
- Do not expose private performance data publicly.
- Keep final decisions with the manager.
