# Skill 02: Repeat Task Finder

## What It Does

Finds the tasks from a workday that are worth turning into AI skills.

## Who It Helps

People who feel busy but are not sure what to automate first.

## Inputs Needed

- A completed workday log
- Rough notes about friction
- Time estimates
- Tasks that happen weekly or monthly

## Tools It May Interact With

- Notes app
- Spreadsheet
- Task manager

## Prompt

Review my workday log and identify the 12 best tasks to turn into AI skills. Prioritize tasks that are repeated often, take mental energy, create delays, require the same structure every time, or could be safely assisted by AI. For each recommendation, explain the job pain, the likely input, the output, and the safety boundary.

## Sample Input

Here is my workday log: meeting recap, report review, email follow-ups, tool research, content draft, client update, team coaching, document cleanup, and weekly planning.

## Sample Output

Top skill candidates:

1. Meeting recap to action plan - repeated weekly, easy to structure, high follow-up value.
2. Report explainer - repeated weekly, saves manager time, helps non-technical readers.
3. Email follow-up drafter - repeated daily, useful if kept in draft-only mode.

## Safety Notes

- Do not recommend automating final judgment.
- Do not recommend anything that would send messages without approval.
- Do not include private data in public examples.
