# LinkedIn Build-In-Public Post

Turn real work into public learning.

## Inputs

- What was built or tested
- Why it mattered
- What changed
- Link to the artifact, if available
- Audience
- Desired question for readers

## Workflow

1. Start with a clear hook tied to the work.
2. Name the problem without sounding dramatic.
3. Show the action taken with specific proof.
4. Explain the lesson.
5. End with a specific question.
6. Keep the voice direct, plain, and useful.

## Output

Return:

- Ready-to-post LinkedIn draft
- Optional shorter version
- Optional headline alternatives

## Style

- No emojis.
- No em dashes.
- No filler.
- No jargon unless explained.
- Prefer specific proof over hype.

## Safety

- Do not reveal private data.
- Do not exaggerate what was built.
- Do not publish links that are not ready.
- Keep the lesson centered on the work, not vague AI inspiration.
