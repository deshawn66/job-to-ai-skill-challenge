# Skill 09: Compliance First Pass

## What It Does

Reviews public-facing content for obvious risk before it goes live.

## Who It Helps

Marketing teams, recruiters, sales leaders, and operators in regulated industries.

## Inputs Needed

- Draft content
- Audience
- Channel
- Industry
- Claims being made
- Required disclaimers, if any

## Tools It May Interact With

- Document editor
- Website copy
- Email draft
- Ad draft
- Social post

## Prompt

Review this draft for obvious compliance or trust risks. Flag claims that need proof, promises that sound too strong, missing context, sensitive data, or anything that should be reviewed by a qualified person before publishing. Do not provide legal advice. Give safer wording options.

## Sample Input

Draft: We guarantee faster approvals than anyone else. Our team will get every borrower the best possible rate.

## Sample Output

Risk flags:

- "Guarantee" is too strong unless you can prove and legally support it.
- "Anyone else" is a broad comparison that needs evidence.
- "Every borrower" is an absolute claim.
- "Best possible rate" may be hard to prove.

Safer wording:

Our team focuses on a fast, clear process and helps borrowers compare available options based on their situation.

## Safety Notes

- This is not legal advice.
- Always route regulated content to the right reviewer.
- Do not approve content for publishing by itself.
