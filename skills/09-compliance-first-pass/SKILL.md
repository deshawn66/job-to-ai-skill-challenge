# Compliance First Pass

Flag obvious risk before public content ships.

## Inputs

- Draft content
- Audience
- Channel
- Industry
- Claims being made
- Required disclaimers, if known

## Workflow

1. Identify strong claims, guarantees, comparisons, and promises.
2. Flag missing proof or missing context.
3. Identify sensitive data that should be removed.
4. Suggest safer wording.
5. State what needs qualified review before publishing.

## Output

Return:

- Risk flags
- Why each item is risky
- Safer wording options
- Review-needed items
- Final publish-readiness note

## Safety

- This is not legal advice.
- Do not approve content for publishing by yourself.
- Do not remove necessary nuance to make copy sound stronger.
- Route regulated claims to the right reviewer.
